# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors, regardless of experience level, background, or identity. 

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**

✅ Being respectful and considerate  
✅ Accepting constructive criticism gracefully  
✅ Focusing on what's best for the project  
✅ Showing empathy towards others  

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**

❌ Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks  
❌ Publishing others' private information  
❌ Spam or off-topic discussions  
❌ Any conduct that violates professional ethics  

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to: **constantine.ctf@proton.me**

We will review and investigate all complaints and respond appropriately.
